# Retail Sales & Revenue Dashboard (Power BI)

## Business Objective
To provide business stakeholders with a centralized view of retail sales performance,
revenue growth, and profitability across regions, stores, and product categories.

## Dataset Overview
- Sales Transactions
- Product Master
- Store Master
- Date Dimension

## Data Modeling
- Star schema design
- FactSales connected to Product, Store, and Date dimensions
- Optimized relationships for performance

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- YoY & MoM Growth
- Gross Margin %
- Average Order Value
- Top / Bottom Performing Stores

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- SQL (data preparation)

## Business Value
Enabled management to quickly identify underperforming regions and
optimize sales strategies based on data-driven insights.
